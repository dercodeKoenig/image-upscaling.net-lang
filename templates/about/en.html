FAQ

Q: Is the API free to use?

Yes and no. It has the same free quota limits that you have on this website. You can make a donation to claim extra quota here.

Q: My request is not being processed?

Sometimes there's a queue due to high demand since several tools share the same server. Usually, it works after a few minutes. If it's still stuck after 10 minutes, something might be wrong (e.g., power failure, server crash). Please report it via email or Discord.
